# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Football fans following the 2026 FIFA World Cup Round of 16. They want an interactive, fun way to predict match outcomes — not through traditional forms, but through a gamified scratch card experience. Context: casual social sharing, excitement-building before matches, used on desktop and mobile devices during watch parties or solo viewing.

## Product Purpose
An interactive scratch card prediction game for the 2026 World Cup Round of 16. Users select a match, then scratch cells on an N×N grid to reveal predicted scores. The score distribution follows real match probability data. Each match has its own independent prediction page. The goal: turn match prediction into a moment of suspense and delight.

## Brand Personality
Exciting, Fun, Sporty — captures the thrill of the World Cup with a playful gamified twist. The interface should feel like opening a real scratch card: anticipation, reveal, reaction.

## Anti-references
- Generic gambling/betting sites with cluttered interfaces, aggressive color schemes, and overwhelming information density
- Overly plain or corporate-looking sports apps
- Cartoonish or childish game interfaces

## Design Principles
1. **Suspense-first interaction** — every scratch should feel like a mini-reveal moment
2. **Clean excitement** — sporty energy without gambling-site clutter
3. **One match, one focus** — each match gets its own dedicated prediction space
4. **Touch-friendly** — scratch cards must feel natural on both mouse and touch
5. **Instant gratification** — results are immediate and satisfying

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA compliance for all text and interactive elements
- Reduced motion alternative for scratch animations
- Color-blind safe team identifiers (always paired with text labels, not color alone)
- Touch targets ≥ 44px for mobile scratch interaction
